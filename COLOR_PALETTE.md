# ALYAI Color Palette

## Overview
The app's color scheme is inspired by the concentric ring logo, representing emotional, mental, and physical support.

## Primary Colors

### Core Lavender (Primary)
- **Hex**: `#C9A8E8`
- **Usage**: Main brand color, buttons, accents
- **Represents**: The AI core companion

### Emotional Support (Soft Purple)
- **Hex**: `#D4B5F0`
- **Usage**: Emotional support indicators, Mind & Emotions section
- **Represents**: Inner emotional well-being

### Mental Support (Calm Blue)
- **Hex**: `#B8D8E8`
- **Usage**: Mental wellness features, Medical Companion section
- **Represents**: Mental clarity and support

### Physical Support (Gentle Green)
- **Hex**: `#A8D8C8`
- **Usage**: Physical health features, Nutrition & Movement sections
- **Represents**: Physical vitality and wellness

## Light Accents

- **Light Purple**: `#E8D4F8` - Backgrounds, subtle highlights
- **Light Blue**: `#D4E8F8` - Secondary backgrounds
- **Light Green**: `#C8E8D8` - Tertiary backgrounds

## Gradients

### Primary Gradient
- From: Core Lavender (`#C9A8E8`)
- To: Emotional Purple (`#D4B5F0`)
- **Usage**: Main buttons, hero sections, key icons

### Mental Gradient
- From: Calm Blue (`#B8D8E8`)
- To: Light Blue (`#D4E8F8`)
- **Usage**: Mental health features

### Physical Gradient
- From: Gentle Green (`#A8D8C8`)
- To: Light Green (`#C8E8D8`)
- **Usage**: Physical wellness features

## Color-Coded Features

### Support Categories
- **Nutrition & Meals**: Green (Physical)
- **Movement & Energy**: Green (Physical)
- **Mind & Emotions**: Purple (Emotional)
- **Medical Companion**: Blue (Mental)

## Implementation

All colors are defined in `ColorTheme.swift` as SwiftUI Color extensions:
- `.alyaiPrimary`
- `.alyaiEmotional`
- `.alyaiMental`
- `.alyaiPhysical`
- `.alyaiGradient`
- And more...

## Design Philosophy

The color palette embodies ALYAI's holistic approach:
- **Calming**: Soft, muted tones reduce anxiety
- **Welcoming**: Warm gradients create emotional safety
- **Intelligent**: Sophisticated colors convey professionalism
- **Adaptive**: Different hues for different support types
